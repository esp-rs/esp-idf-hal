Expand description

@brief Structure representing a report entry for Fine Timing Measurement (FTM) in Wi-Fi.

This structure holds the information related to the FTM process between a Wi-Fi FTM Initiator and a Wi-Fi FTM Responder. FTM is used for precise distance measurement by timing the exchange of frames between devices.

§dlog_token: u8

< Dialog Token of the FTM frame

§rssi: i8

< RSSI of the FTM frame received

§rtt: u32

< Round Trip Time in pSec with a peer

§t1: u64

< Time of departure of FTM frame from FTM Responder in pSec

§t2: u64

< Time of arrival of FTM frame at FTM Initiator in pSec

§t3: u64

< Time of departure of ACK from FTM Initiator in pSec

§t4: u64

< Time of arrival of ACK at FTM Responder in pSec
